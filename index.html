About NMACC

The Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre (NMACC) is a first-of-its-kind, multi-disciplinary space in the sphere of arts, located in the heart of Mumbai, India. Since its opening in March 2023, the Cultural Centre has staged more than 1,500 shows featuring over 7,000 artists across venues, welcoming a total of more than two million guests. NMACC has been featured in the international publication Madame Figaro as one of the seven leading cultural centres of the world.

The first edition of the NMACC India Weekend takes forward the vision of bringing the best of India to the world with a three-day cultural showcase! The one-of-its-kind event celebrates the finest of Indian performing arts, fashion, music, wellness, and cuisine with a host of exciting presentations. NMACC India Weekend is a three-day cultural showcase celebrating the finest of Indian performing arts, fashion, music, wellness, and cuisine. It will be held from September 12–14, 2025 at David H. Koch Theater and Damrosch Park,Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, New York City.
NMACC India Weekend will take place from September 12-14, 2025 at David H. Koch Theater and Damrosch Park, Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, New York City. We look forward to welcoming you!
The NMACC India Weekend will premiere ‘The Great Indian Musical: Civilization to Nation,’ India’s largest ever theatrical production – amongst other thoughtfully curated cultural presentations. It will also feature a ‘Great Indian Bazaar’ - where guests will be introduced to the finest Indian fashion and textiles, authentic Indian flavours, as well as dance, yoga and music experiences.
India's largest theatrical production ‘The Great Indian Musical: Civilization to Nation’ is a spectacular tribute to India’s past, present and future —blending dance, art, fashion, and music. It travels the country’s history from 5000 BC until its independence in 1947.
The musical is an NMACC presentation, conceived and directed by Feroz Abbas Khan, with music by Ajay-Atul, choreography by Mayuri Upadhya, Vaibhavi Merchant, Samir & Arsh Tanna, and costumes by Manish Malhotra. .
The show will have a limited run of five performances. The production features a cast of over 100 performers, stunning costumes, and grand sets. We look forward to welcoming you!
